SaaS and Tech-Enabled Services Valuation Model
An institutional-grade, browser-based valuation tool for evaluating SaaS companies, built to replicate frameworks used in private equity and M&A diligence.
Core modules:

EV/ARR multiple build-up with 9 source-cited adjustments
Full DCF schedule from Revenue through Free Cash Flow
ARR Bridge waterfall with NRR reconciliation
Cohort retention curves modeled over 7 years
Sensitivity heatmaps with full model recomputed per cell
20-item weighted diligence scorecard

All benchmarks sourced from SEG, SaaS Capital, Aventis Advisors, and Benchmarkit.
